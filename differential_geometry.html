Complex Geometry Notes

Basics of smooth manifolds: inverse function theorem, implicit function theorem, submanifolds, integration on manifolds.


Basics of matric Lie groups over R and C: definitions of GLn, SUn, SOn, Un, their manifold structures, Lie algebras, right and left invariant vector fields and differential forms, the exponential map.


Definition of real and complex vector bundles, tangent and cotangent bundles, basic operations on bundles such as dual bundle, tensor products, direct sums, pull-back bundles.


Definition of differential forms, exterior product, exterior derivative, de Rham cohomology, behaviour under pull-back.


Metrics on vector bundles.


Riemannian metrics, definition of geodesic, existence and uniqueness of geodesics.


Definition of a principal Lie group bundle for matrix groups.


Associated vector bundles: relation between principle bundles and vector bundles.


Definition of covariant derivative for a vector bundle and connection to principal bundle.


Definition of curvature, flat connections, parallel transport.


Definition of Levi-Civita connection and properties of Riemann curvature tensor.
